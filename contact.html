Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

We offer fast shipping options. Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days, while expedited shipping delivers within 2-3 business days to most locations.

What's your return policy?

We're confident in our quality. All items can be returned within 30 days of purchase in original condition for a full refund or exchange.

Do you offer customization?

Yes! Many of our pieces can be customized with your initials or specific requests. Contact us for details and pricing on custom orders.

Is Street Code sizing true to fit?

Our sizing is accurate to standard measurements. We provide a detailed size guide with every product. When in doubt, contact our support team.

How do I join the community?

Simply make a purchase or sign up for our newsletter. All members get exclusive access to events, drops, and community forums.

Can I collaborate with Street Code?

We love collaborations! If you're an artist, influencer, or creator, email us at partnerships@streetcode.com with your proposal.
